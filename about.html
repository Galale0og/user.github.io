About Me

A few years ago, if i told someone i was thinking off a career in web development, they'd have laughed at me. Came outta nowhere.

But when it did, it all clicked. I started using Mimo when i was at home, in a house with no internet to code, or use codecademy whenever i was online. I started to work on my very own blog. Not much for little old me, i haven't got too much to show for right now. The journey to change that begins....
